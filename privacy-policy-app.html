Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the WA Drivers Test mobile application ("App", "we", "our", or "us") handles your information.

Summary: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The App does not:

  • Require account creation or login
  • Collect your name, email, or contact information
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies

Data Storage

All data related to your use of the App (such as quiz progress and whether you've accepted the terms) is stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never transmitted to external servers
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the App

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar analytics tools
  • Advertising networks or ad SDKs
  • Social media integrations
  • Crash reporting services that collect personal data

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, children's privacy is inherently protected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
